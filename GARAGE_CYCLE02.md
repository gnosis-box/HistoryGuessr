**HistoryGuessr** is a Circles-native guessing game where players discover historical clues and try to identify the right person, place, event, or period. For this cycle, judges should test the main game flow, then explore the **Circles social layer** (trusted circles, community quizzes, trust-graph duels) and the **HIST economy** — History Guessr’s on-chain **group currency** on Gnosis.

### What is HIST?

**HIST** is not a fake in-game point. It is History Guessr’s **Circles group currency**: merit for learning, verification, and curation inside our circle. Personal **CRC** is protocol money on Gnosis; **HIST** is how the game scores cultural contribution before value can flow back through Circles group rules.

**How players earn HIST (in the miniapp):**

1. Connect via the Circles playground (wallet in the header).
2. Play any challenge mode (map, timeline, sources, duels, etc.).
3. Strong runs (**score ≥ 400**) credit HIST to an in-app ledger (fair daily cap while we scale).
4. Open the **HIST** nav tab for the full flywheel: Play → Earn HIST → Trust the group → Redeem CRC → Reinvest in the circle.

**How HIST connects to Circles (on-chain):**

1. Support / trust the **History Guessr** group in the [Circles app](https://app.aboutcircles.com) to hold and move group tokens.
2. **HIST ↔ CRC** conversion follows standard [group currency](https://docs.aboutcircles.com/overview/how-it-works/group-currencies) mechanics: group tokens redeem against **treasury collateral** (CRC operators deposit) — not a fixed “1 HIST = X €” button in the miniapp.
3. Trust paths (e.g. Gnosis Group anchor) gate full reward eligibility — aligned with Circles’ trust graph, not anonymous leaderboards.

**Giving HIST** to someone is **recognition** inside the group economy (same design as Circles group transfers); duels and leaderboards preview social distribution among people you trust.

### Suggested demo path

1. Open the app through the **Circles playground** (link above).
2. Connect wallet — confirm header shows your Circles session.
3. Play a **HistoryGuessr** round (any category; try **Friend Challenge** or a map mode).
4. Open **HIST** — read “How it works” and “Your HIST” (ledger / trust status).
5. Open **Circles** hub — overview → **Trusted friends** (trust graph) or **Circles & quizzes**.
6. Create or open a **community**, publish or play a shared quiz, copy an invite link.
7. Optional: in the Circles app, support the History Guessr **HIST** group and skim group-currency docs.

**Known limitations:** some flows are prototype-level (in-app ledger may run ahead of on-chain batch payouts; community storage is local-first). Optimized for hackathon review rather than production usage.

## What changed this cycle *

Cycle 02 expands HistoryGuessr beyond a single guessing-game loop.

**Historical discovery & communities**

- Stronger historical discovery layer and cleaner navigation.
- **Circles hub**: create or explore **trusted circles**, build **custom quizzes**, share portable invite links, and challenge **trust-graph peers** on the map.
- Players can gather around shared historical interests — not only isolated clues.

**HIST — Circles group currency (new for judges)**

- Deployed **HIST** group currency on Gnosis (History Guessr group token).
- In-app **earn → ledger → claim** flow tied to quest scores and daily fairness caps.
- Dedicated **HIST** screen explaining the economy: earn by playing, trust the group in Circles, redeem HIST against treasury **CRC** collateral.
- Trust-gated rewards and vouching aligned with Circles primitives (Gnosis Group anchor, supporter status on the HIST group).
- **Circles** tab consolidates social play (duels, circles, redeem) for non-crypto-readable UX.

Together, this moves the project toward a **Circles-native social game**: historical curiosity, community challenges, trust-based competition, and a real group-currency loop — not just a quiz.
